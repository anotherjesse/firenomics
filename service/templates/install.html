Installation

To get started you have to install another extension! By installing our extension we will (eventually) automatically update your extension list whenever you install, uninstall or disable an extension.

Install by clicking here.

Currently updating is manual, so after restarting your browser, visit your extension list to make sure you are logged in, then visit chrome://firenomics/content/submit.html manually using your URL Bar and the submit button on that page. It will finish in second, come back and visit your extension list...
